Jolly App User Interface


Jolly is a diet-friendly food delivery application. I designed its user interface based on my experience when I tried to eat healthier. I used Adobe Illustrator to create the banner, icon, menu bar, etc. I made 4 pages that will be consisted of the app, which are the Home page, explore, Order Plan page, and food arrival notification page.
